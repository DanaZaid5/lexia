Lexia uses a transparent, rule-based system to score how hard any English word is to read — built to support research in dyslexia and literacy.

Literacy Research

Reproducible difficulty scores to support academic work in reading comprehension and vocabulary acquisition.

Fully Transparent

Every score is explained. Each rule is named and inspectable — no black-box logic.

Modular Rules Engine

Rules can be added, removed, or changed independently without touching the interface.

Dyslexia-Aware

Rules are grounded in phonological complexity and irregular spelling patterns that affect dyslexic readers.

Scoring rules
16 independent rules are applied to every word. Each contributes a numeric score to the total.
